ACADIA® NATURAL

Specifications:


18 LB to 50 LB (24 x 36-500)


Natural OGR is an oil and grease resistant paper custom designed to meet the needs of individual customers. Please contact us to discuss your specification requirements.

MEDICAL PACKAGING

Specifications:


8 LB to 30 LB (24 x 36-500)


This base paper can be laminated to foil for blister packaging applications including over-the-counter and prescription medications, vitamins and/or supplements.

DELI & BAKERY PAPERS

Specifications:


10 LB to 25 LB (24 x 36-500)


An MG base paper that is best used for converting to waxed interfolded sheets. As deli wax paper manufacturers, we provide bakery papers used for storage and wrap.

ALLIANCE® RELEASE

Specifications:


40 & 42 LB (24 x 36-500)


Alliance® TT is a propreitary formulation and smoothness to deliver high-quality image reproduction. A pressure-sensitive face stock, Alliance TT features a blue-white shade that provides superior contrast for bar code readability.

BAG KRAFT WET STRENGTH

Specifications:


40 – 60 lb. (lbs./3000 ft2)


Engineered for Quick, Clean Filling and Rigorous Handling of Multiwall bags. As the go-to choice for multiwall bags, this unbleached machine-finished sack paper is designed for performance in high-speed filling lines.

POROUS MULTIWALL

Specifications:


40 & 42 LB (24 x 36-500)


With exceptional a porous surface and strength characteristics, this unbleached, machine-finished sack paper is manufactured with an open surface and high tear resistance to optimize converting and filling line efficiency.
